# Privacy Policy for URL Phishing Detector

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

## 1. Introduction
URL Phishing Detector ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension and backend service handle your data.

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect and process the following data:
- **URLs**: The web addresses (URLs) of the pages you visit are sent to our backend server for analysis.
- **Technical Data**: We may collect basic technical information such as your browser version and extension version for debugging purposes.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
- **Phishing Detection**: We analyze the URLs you visit to determine if they are safe, suspicious, or malicious.
- **Service Improvement**: We may use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our detection algorithms.

## 4. Data Retention
- **URLs**: URLs sent for analysis are processed in real-time. We do not store your browsing history permanently.
- **Logs**: Temporary server logs may be kept for up to 7 days for security and debugging purposes.

## 5. Third-Party Services

- **Hosting Provider**: Our backend is hosted on [Render/AWS/etc.].
- **WHOIS & DNS**: We query public WHOIS and DNS records to verify domain information.
